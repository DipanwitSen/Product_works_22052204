To provide a seamless, trustworthy, and feature-rich platform for local entrepreneurs to showcase their products. Customers can find unique, locally sourced items with ease and confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I become a seller on LocalMart?

Sign up and select the 'Seller' role during registration. Once activated, access your Seller Dashboard to start listing products.

What is the chatbot's purpose?

Our AI chatbot provides immediate support, answering common questions about ordering, shipping, returns, and product info.

How do I reset my password?

On login, click 'Forgot Password', enter your email, and you'll receive a unique PIN to verify your identity and reset safely.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are securely processed via Razorpay, supporting credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and more.

What is your policy on shipping and returns?

Shipping is managed by individual sellers. Check each product page for delivery details. Returns handled directly with sellers or contact support if needed.
